Directions

The Nonantum is about 90 minutes north of Boston / Less than 5 hours from New York City.

Take I-95 North to Exit #19 in Maine (Wells).
Take a left on Route 9.
Go 1.5 miles and take a left onto Route 1 North.
Drive 1 mile and take a right back onto Route 9.
After 4 miles, cross the small bridge into Kennebunkport and Dock Square.
Take a right in Dock square at the Center Pillar onto Ocean Avenue.
The Nonantum Resort is 0.7 mile on the right.
