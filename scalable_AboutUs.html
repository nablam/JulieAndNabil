Julie and Nabil

Julie and Nabil met in January 2012 during a ski weekend in Sugarloaf, ME. The rest is history.

Since then, they have shared everything. Their common interest in travel and the outdoors took them on many adventures around the world such as camping trips and lighthouse sightings in Maine, snowboarding/skiing in Whistler, BC, snorkeling and sunbathing in St Maarten, enjoying amazing foods and wine in France, and many more.

Julie and Nabil got engaged in August 2014 on the small island of Vinalhaven, ME at the end of one of their Maine summer road trips. A wedding in beautiful Kennebunkport on the seacoast of Maine was a logical choice given the nature of their relationship with the State of Maine.

Julie and Nabil live with their cat Pasha in Somerville, MA and are looking forward to continuing to share their lives together. They are both very happy that you can join their union, either in person or in spirit.
