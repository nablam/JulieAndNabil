Things to do in the area

If you have a day or less:
In the Kennebunks / walking distance

  • Shopping in Dock square (Kennebunkport, ME)
  • Drive up to the George Bush Sr. Compound (Walker’s Point)
  • Kayak in the Kennebunk River
  • Walk along Kennebunk beach

A short drive away (under an hour)
  • Walk along Marginal Way in Ogunquit, ME (scenic walk along the coast) – (~30 min drive south)
  • Cape Neddick Lighthouse (most photographed lighthouse in the US) (~40 min drive south)
  • Outlets of Kittery, ME (~40 min drive south)

If you have 2-3 days
  • Visit Boston, MA
  • Drive up North on the coast of Maine
  • Newport, RI

If you have more than 3 days
  • Provincetown / Cape Cod / Martha’s Vineyard / Nantucket
  • Acadia National Park
  • White Mountains
  • New York City, NY
